SpaceX Website Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2024

We don’t knowingly sell your personal information, but we still have to tell you what we collect from you and how we use it. We may update this policy from time to time. Your continued use of this site indicates your agreement to the updated terms.

If you are a prospective, current or past Starlink customer, this Privacy Policy does not apply to you—please refer instead to our Starlink Privacy Policy.

1. What Personal Information Do We Collect?

  • Identity Data: first name, last name, title.
  • Contact Data: postal address, email address, telephone numbers.
  • Profile Data: username, password, purchases, customer service requests.
  • Financial Data: payment card details, billing address.
  • Transaction Data: payments and purchase details.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser info, location, OS, platform, website interactions.
  • Communication Data: preferences, uploaded files, inferences from other data.
  • Employment Data: if applying for a job with SpaceX, including work history, education, contact info.

2. How Do We Collect Personal Information?

  • Directly from You: account registration, product/service requests, subscriptions, correspondence.
  • From Third Parties: service providers, subcontractors, search providers, prior employment data.
  • Cookies & Online Tracking: cookies, server logs, device identifiers; for logged-in and non-logged-in users. More info at CookiePro.

3. How and Why Do We Use Personal Information?

  • Provide goods and services
  • Process and deliver orders
  • Manage payments, fees, and charges
  • Manage our relationship with you
  • Protect our business, detect fraud, enforce policies
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Deliver relevant news and offers
  • Assess employment applications

4. With Whom Do We Disclose Personal Information?

  • Service Providers: affiliates, analytics, hosting, backup, customer service.
  • Government & Advisors: regulators, courts, professional advisors.
  • Business Transfers: mergers, acquisitions, reorganizations.
  • Third parties with your consent.

5. How Do We Protect Personal Information?

We implement technological, physical, and administrative measures to protect your data. While we strive for security, no method is perfect and we cannot guarantee complete protection.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You may have rights to access, update, or erase your data. For US residents, review our US State Privacy Notice. For EEA+ residents, review the EEA+ Privacy Notice. Opt-out instructions via privacy@spacex.com.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child submitted data, contact us at privacy@spacex.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary or required by law.

9. Third-Party Websites

We are not responsible for third-party websites linked from ours. Their policies may differ, and your data may be handled differently.
